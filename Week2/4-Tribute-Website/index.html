Biography

İhsan Oktay Anar is a Turkish writer known for his historical fiction novels with elements of fantasy and philosophy. His works often incorporate rich Ottoman-era storytelling, blending myth, history, and fiction.

Born in 1960, he studied philosophy and later became a lecturer. His debut novel, *Puslu Kıtalar Atlası*, gained cult status for its unique storytelling and literary style.
